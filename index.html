About me

University as the beginning of my story...

I am a student of the University of Pretoria. I began my studies in the University of Pretoria on Mamelodi campus in the BCom extended program. It was the case for many of us that ended up there, that we had underperformed with respect to the different degrees we intended on studying and as a result got into the extended program. We that come from Mamelodi are known for being hardworking and achievers, to bear testament to that fact I was invited to become a part of the Golden Key International Honor Society.

A sporty cultured person!

During my high school days I was very sports centered, I am an athlete and would often represent the Free State Province at national competitions. I still do sports and intent to continue to do so until I complete my degree. Also on being a woman of culture, I enjoy tailoring, I might become a seamtress.

Where am I going you ask?

I see myself traveling abroad for work. I have always dreamt of doing so. I would like to be nomadic in my career, work with the best people in the best places is what I want my life to be all about. On that note, If tuks has an opening for being an AL or Tutor, please let me know. Thank you!
